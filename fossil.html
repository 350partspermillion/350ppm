The mission of 350partspermillion is to illustrate the changes of the past, present, and future that represent our relationship with the environment.

The second design, the Fossil Print, appears in the form of the 350 logo. The "Fossil" moniker comes from the fact that plant-based charcoal gives the print its grey color. This 350 printed in carbon is a monument to the safety of the past, menace of the present, and chaos of the future.
